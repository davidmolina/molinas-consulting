<p class="intro"><span class="dropcap">P</span>roblem: Dispatch was manual and slow; the owner was the bottleneck for calls, emails, and job routing.</p>

<p><a href="{{ '/case-studies/hvac-dispatch-automation/' | relative_url }}">HVAC systems</a> teams pursuing <a href="{{ '/case-studies/hvac-dispatch-automation/' | relative_url }}">contractor automation</a> or a streamlined <a href="{{ '/case-studies/hvac-dispatch-automation/' | relative_url }}">dispatch workflow</a> can reference this build as a playbook.</p>

<p><strong>Outcome:</strong> Reduced response time from hours to minutes through automated intake and dispatch routing.</p>

## Action
- Rebuilt the website intake to capture and route service requests.
- Deployed a dispatch workflow (app + SOPs) so techs get jobs without owner intervention.
- Documented SOPs to lock in the process, training, and QA.

## Outcome
- Response times dropped from hours to minutes with team-wide visibility.
- Consistent inbound from the site to the dispatch queue.
- Owner time freed for growth work instead of call handling.
