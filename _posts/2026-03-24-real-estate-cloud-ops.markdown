<p class="intro"><span class="dropcap">P</span>roblem: Disconnected tools, unclear roles, and slow follow-up on opportunities.</p>

<p><strong>Outcome:</strong> Unified cloud workspace with clear accountability and faster follow-up, eliminating data loss.</p>

## Action
- Mapped decision rights and operating cadence across teams.
- Standardized cloud tooling (docs, CRM, comms) with access controls.
- Set up reporting pipelines for pipeline health and task ownership.

## Outcome
- Clear accountability and faster follow-up on leads.
- Unified workspace reduced context switching.
- Leadership visibility into throughput and blockers.
